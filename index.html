Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranbarries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized Onions ,tart cranberries and creamie brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranbarries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin oil

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desire, though it`s not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh ore frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with pddle attachment, mix together all the dough Ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don`t need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the lst of the flour...
